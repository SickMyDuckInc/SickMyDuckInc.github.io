Projects

GateCrasher is a tower-defense style game in which you must place your troops in scene the best possible way to stop heaven invaders before they manage to get in.

You will only get one chance to decide your stratey, as from the minute you press play you wont be able to make any changes. How many times will you have to play each level to beat it? ¿Will you be able to get all three stars?

Balaclava is an asimetric multiplayer game in which you and a friend will have to steal all the money in a random bank.

One of you will be inside the bank doing the dirty job. The other one will be hacking into the bank database, trying to guide the thief on the process. Will you be able to do the job in time?

Eddie is a diver. Eddie wants to go down into the ocean. Eddie dies. Eddie starts again.

In Dive Eddie & Die you will put yourself in Eddie's shoes and try to get as far down into the ocen as you can, collecting as many coins as possible and beating the dreaful fishes that you encounter along the way. If you catch a fish with your bubble gun you will be able to transform into it for a few seconds. ¿Dare to try?

The inmensity of the ocean is full of dangers. Sharks and other terrible fishes stalk in the dark.

In Un Poisson Andalou you will be an abyssal fish who has to survive this dangers; dodge sharks and ocean trash and pick up bonuses to gain light and speed while you go as far as you possibly can.
